What We Do

Hospital Service

Trinetra Advanced Eye Care Hospital, Lucknow offers winning ophthalmic eye care services, with a combination of science and personalized care for patients with routine and complex eye conditions. Backed by three storied facility, we are at the forefront of the development and clinical adoption of innovative ophthalmological technologies that are transforming the field. Trinetra has been a leader in clinical practice and research since its inception.

Today we continue to bring the most advanced surgical and micro-interventional procedures, as well as the latest diagnostic and therapeutic services to our patients, with the goal of delivering sight-saving interventions, safely and quickly. Our commitment to patient care and excellence has been consistently recognized by Quality Council of India, which ranks Trinetra as having one of the city’s best ophthalmology programs. Trinetra's eye specialists diagnose and treat hundreds of eye conditions, including many that are rare or complex. We offer a full range of eye care, from routine eye exams to surgery. The health of your eyes and your experience in our hospital is important to us.
